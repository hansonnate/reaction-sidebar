What is Contact Cleaner?

Contact Cleaner was made to help clean up audience lists that are used for large email sendouts. Manually checking each person in a list of contacts is a pain, especially when that list is over 10,000 contacts. Contact Cleaner makes it easy to upload, adjust, and fix those bad contacts found within large lists.

Why clean your audience?

When working with large lists the emails that are missing the domains, @ symbols, and have mispelled domains do not recieve the emails you are wanting to send. Also people (like doctors) get upset when they do not have their correct prefix or just have something wrong with their name. The end goal of contact cleaner is to fix all these issues before disaster strikes.
